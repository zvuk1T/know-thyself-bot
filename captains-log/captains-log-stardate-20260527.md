## ✅ Notes for Students

This session had no code. That is intentional — and worth noting.

The most common mistake when starting an AI project is opening a code editor before
the architecture is clear. You end up refactoring everything three times.

The questions we answered today — What is this for? Who is it for? Where does the data live?
What are the risks? — these are architecture questions. They take 30 minutes to answer
in a conversation. They take 3 weeks to fix if you skip them and go straight to code.

Design before build. Always. Even when you are excited. Especially when you are excited.
